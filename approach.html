OUR APPROACH

The only way to solve a problem is to understand it from every angle. Luckily, we’ve got
a proven approach to satisfy our insatiable curiosity.

Every project at ACode, small or big, goes through our proven, agile work process.

1. Idea


Digital victories start with ideas. We help you formulate your thoughts into a winning digital model. That includes having a full array of brain power at your disposal to work out every aspect of your idea. During the Idea phase, we:

✓ Engage our CTO and development team to understand what technology works best, what are all the programming languages that apply and formulate the initial design thinking;

✓ Deploy our digital consulting knowledge to understand what works best for the customer and what are the applicable cutting-edge technologies;

✓ Work with you to clearly define the idea, the business model and advise you how to enhance it.

2. Plan


Ideas and projects need plans for execution. We base our
working processes on clear plans for execution of the project.

✓ Defining a budget for the project

✓ Writing a clear specification of design and user interface features which forms a basis for everything we do later

✓ Specifying technologies, programming languages, licences and the end product

✓ Applying agile methodology and defining sprints, deadlines and key project milestones

✓ Outlining our iterations and mutual mode of operations, including your and our project leads

3. Design


The most important part of any development project is
information architecture and user experience design. The
design stage typically involves moving the information outlined
in the planning stage further into reality. The main deliverables
are a documented site structure and, more importantly, a visual
representation.

Our design process includes:

✓ Wireframe and design elements planning

✓ Mock-ups based on requirements analysis

✓ Review and approval cycle

✓ Slice and code valid HTML/CSS

4. Development


Development involves the bulk of the programming work, as
well as loading content. We keep code organized and
commented, and refer constantly to the planning details as the
full website takes shape. Also we take a strategic approach, and
avoid future hassles by constantly testing as we develop.

Our development process includes:

✓ Choosing the development framework

✓ Selecting code templates for each page type

✓ Development and testing of special features and interactivity

✓ Testing and verification of links and functionality

5. Launch


The purpose of the launch phase is to prepare the project for
public viewing. This requires final polishing of design elements,
deep testing of interactivity and features and, most of all, a
consideration of the user experience. An important early step in
this phase is to move the project, if need be, to its permanent
server.

✓ Transfer to live server

✓ Final cross-browser check

✓ Hand off to client

✓ Provision documentation and source files

✓ Project closure, final documentation
